### Essential Accessories
#### Hardwire Kit (Brand Specific)
- **Estimated Cost**: $10.00 - $19.00 (~RM 48 - RM 90) 
- **Purpose**: Connects the dashcam directly to the vehicle's fuse box (12V to 5V step-down) rather than the cigarette lighter.
- **Requirement**: Mandatory if you want the dashcams to utilize their "24-Hour Parking Mode" when the engine is off. Must be bought to match the specific camera brand (e.g., VIOFO HK4 for T130).

#### High-Endurance MicroSD Card
- **Estimated Cost**: ~$20.00 (~RM 95) for 128GB
- **Purpose**: Storage of loop-recorded video files.
- **Requirement**: Must be specifically rated as "High Endurance" or "Max Endurance" (e.g., SanDisk High Endurance, Samsung PRO Endurance). Standard MicroSD cards will fail within weeks due to constant rewrite cycles and high cabin heat.
